About Me

I am a Computer Science and Engineering student at The Ohio State University. I enjoy working at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and real world problem solving. My academic background includes coursework in Data Structures & Algorithms, Object Oriented Programming, Software Development, Discrete Mathematics, Probability and Statistics, Linear Algebra, Calculus and Differential Equations, and Design Thinking. I like bringing these concepts into the projects I work on and using them to build practical ideas.

Right now I am focused on building agentic AI agents that can help solve practical problems in a meaningful way. I am currently working on MoodLogger through the BDAA project series at Ohio State. MoodLogger is a mental health well being web app that helps students track their emotions, practice mindfulness and access personalized support. I am also developing UniConnect, a mobile app that aims to support international students as they move abroad to pursue higher education.

I have completed certifications on Generative AI, LLMs, Responsible AI, Prompt Design, SageMaker and Power BI through NVIDIA, Google Cloud, AWS and Microsoft. I enjoy exploring new ideas in AI and software engineering and I am always looking for ways to keep growing!
